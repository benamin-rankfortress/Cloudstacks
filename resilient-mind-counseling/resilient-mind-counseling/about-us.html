About Us

Resilient Mind Counseling

Resilient Mind Counseling, located in Asheville, North Carolina, is a professional counseling service and mental health clinic. We are proud of you for starting your journey to recovery and for putting yourself first. We provide mental health services for those suffering from substance abuse and addiction with co-occurring disorders -depression, trauma, and many others. Give us a call today to schedule an appointment. The office is located between Merrimon and Charlotte Streets on Clayton Street. Address: 41 Clayton Street Asheville NC 28801 We are now accepting new clients. Blue Cross/Blue Shield, Medcost, Aetna, and United Healthcare insurance are accepted as well as cash pay options.
