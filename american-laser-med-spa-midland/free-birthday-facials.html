WHAT IS COOLSCULPTING?

CoolSculpting is a safe and effective, non-surgical option to contouring the body. The procedure works by utilizing controlled cooling to freeze fat cells, which are then naturally removed from the body by the immune system. In this sense, the fat cells are permanently eliminated, revealing a smoother, toned and contoured body. A CoolSculpting fat reduction procedure is specially designed for those who are at or near their ideal weight but have unwanted stubborn fat.

WHICH AREAS CAN COOLSCULPTING TREAT? - CoolSculpting is effective in targeting and eliminating fat in the following common trouble areas: Love handles, Muffin tops,Bra lines,Flanks, Inner and outer thighs, Saddlebags, Belly fat, Banana roll, or the fat located under the buttocks, Upper arms

WHAT IS THE PROCEDURE LIKE IN AMERICAN LASER MED SPA?

At American Laser Med Spa, we take the time to discuss a holistic approach to your goals for reshaping your body. Together, we’ll look at your whole body from different angles – and then focus on specific areas where you want to eliminate fat.

During the procedure, an applicator will first be administered to the targeted areas, allowing for the tissue to be drawn into the vacuum cup. The cooling process will then take place, causing a decrease in the temperature of the fat cells, leading to their permanent damage. Over the course of the next few weeks, your body’s natural immune system will work towards expelling the damaged fat cells. The procedure generally takes about an hour and causes minimal discomfort, with most patients enjoying a good book or a nap to pass the time.

WHAT HAPPENS POST-TREATMENT?

For the following six months post-treatment, the body continues to naturally expel fat cells as the remaining fat cells condense, delivering long lasting results leading to an improved appearance. Results will become visible four weeks following treatment, however, optimum results will become apparent within 2-3 months post-treatment. Although some patients may only require one session, multiple sessions may be recommended for some individuals in order to achieve optimum results.

There is no downtime associated with a CoolSculpting procedure, allowing patients to return to their daily activities following treatment. However, we do advise for our patients to maintain a healthy diet and exercise routine, as these aspects can help ensure lasting effects. Common side effects may include discomfort, bruising, swelling or soreness at the treated areas, however, these side effects are considered temporary and mild in natural. Such side effects should subside naturally, without the requirement of any medical attention.

WHAT IS LASER HAIR REMOVAL?

Laser hair removal (also known as laser hair reduction) is the process of utilizing specially designed laser technology to permanently remove unwanted hair. This allows the individual the freedom to no longer worry about hair removal methods, such as waxing and shaving, which can cause irritation and leave behind stubbles of hair. With laser hair removal, the results are long lasting, revealing a smoother complexion year-round. Laser hair removal can safely and effectively treat almost any area of the body. Commonly treated areas include: Bikini line, Underarms, Arms, Upper lip, Chin, Face, Brazilian, Legs, Back, Chest, Stomach.

HOW DOES LASER HAIR REMOVAL WORK? - During laser hair removal, a laser emits a gentle beam of light over the area where you want the hair removed. The light energy is then absorbed by the pigment within the hair, where it is converted into heat, effectively destroying the hair follicle, while also avoiding damage to the surrounding skin. The results are long lasting, preventing any future hair growth. Once a hair follicle is destroyed, it cannot regenerate or produce new hair … ever!
