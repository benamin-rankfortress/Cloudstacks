Frequently Asked Questions

What forms of payment are accepted?

We accept MasterCard, Visa, Discover, American Express, debit cards, and Healthcare Savings Accounts (HSA) a Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA) plans. We accept some forms of health insurance. Please contact us to inquire about your insurance carrier. Payment is processed at the end of each session. Your account will be set up with an auto-pay feature using the credit card, debit card or flexible spending account information provided before treatment begins.

How will I communicate with my counselor or coach?

You will receive access to an app you can download on your smart phone, smart pad or computer. You can communicate through the application directly with your counselor/coach, schedule appointments and make changes to your account through a HIPPA secure environment.

Will you share information with my parents or guardian?

If you are under the age of 18, we will ask for a parent or guardian to consent to a minor’s treatment. We may have periodic check-ins with the client’s parent or guardian, and share general treatment plans, coping skills and activities suggested for improvements. They may share feedback on how well the client is responding to treatment. We recommend having joint sessions approximately every 4 to 6 weeks, however, we will arrange individual sessions based on comfort and preference of the client. The client may request the counselor/coach not to share specific information unless it is considered something indicating potential self-harm or harm to others.
