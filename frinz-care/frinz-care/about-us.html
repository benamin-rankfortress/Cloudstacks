About Us

Frinz Care

At Frinz Care, we are a trusted source for comprehensive mental health counseling services for teens, adults, couples and families. We offer a range of specialized therapies, including counseling for depression, anxiety, neurodivergent disorders (ADHD, Autism), PTSD, addictions, behavior and relationship management and more. We have a highly experienced and qualified team of licensed counselors. And we have Christian counselors if you would like to incorporate Christian values into your therapy, available upon request.We provide virtual counseling you can access from the comfort of your home via a secure telehealth platform at flexible and convenient times to accommodate your schedule. We enable you to live a healthy and happy life!
