What type of tile materials are available?

How much does it cost to tile a kitchen?

Same concepts apply to specific areas such as the kitchen. for example a 10 x 10 kitchen could cost anywhere from $500 to $2000 depending on the materials. different materials have varying difficulty in terms of installing them as well.

What is the cost to install porcelain or ceramic tiles?

If you’re looking to install these tiles for flooring, including labor and materials could cost between $5 to $20 and the price will vary for different areas such as the backsplashes or the coutertops which can go up to $32.

How much does it cost to tile a bathroom?

Bathrooms are popular places to retile for your home. It is usually recommended to retile your bathroom every 5 years depending on the maintenance done and the condition. The material is a big factor but typically on average it will cost $5 to $20 per square foot on your standard tile materials. Give us a call today to find out which one is the best fit for you!

How much does is cost to tile a house?

You can generally lay different types of materials anywhere in the house and the typical raw material usually cost anywhere between $3 to $7 per square foor depending on the materials you choose. If including the cost of installation, you should expect to include additional $5 to $10 per square foot.
