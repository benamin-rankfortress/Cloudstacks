About Us

Good Time Tours

Good Time Tours offers Lake Travis party boat rentals with captains in Austin, TX. Come spend a day with us on our pontoon party boats and party barges. Our captained boat rentals are perfect for hosting boat charters for bachelor or bachelorette parties, anniversaries, birthdays, sunset cruises, or just to have a good time with friends on Lake Travis in Austin Texas. Our luxurious, clean, double-decker pontoon party boats can accommodate up to either 17 or 11 people. The boats have 2000 W stereo systems, lilypads, slides, and huge ice chests. You just need to bring your food and drinks, and we will take care of the rest. Contact us today to book a pontoon party boat rental.
