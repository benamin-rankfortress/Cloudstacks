About Us

Shark Plumbing Services

Shark Plumbing Services is a licensed, bonded and insured plumbing company serving residential and commercial customers in San Jose, Redwood City, and the Silicon Valley area. With over 30 years of combined experience, our mission is to become your go-to plumbing team by providing high-quality workmanship and friendly customer service at affordable rates. When a plumbing issue arises, give us a call and our Sharks will be right on the case! We're not a company that aims to just do the bare minimum. Instead, we prefer to go the extra mile in everything we do, because we know that's what keeps customers happy and calling us back. Our top priority is your safety and comfort.
