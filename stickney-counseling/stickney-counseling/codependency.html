CODEPENDENCY

The Client-Centered Approach of Stickney Counseling - Our approach in couples therapy is grounded in understanding and addressing the unique needs of every couple we work with. We believe that each relationship has its own dynamics and challenges, and there’s no one-size-fits-all solution for communication problems. Our therapists facilitate open and honest conversations, acting as neutral mediators to help both partners communicate effectively, identify underlying issues, and develop strategies to enhance their relationship.

Personalized Treatment Plans for Couples at Stickney Counseling - Recognizing that each couple is unique, we tailor our therapy sessions to meet your specific needs. Each couple’s communication issues stem from a variety of factors, including emotional patterns and differing communication styles. We use techniques like “I” statements and active listening, as well as strategies like the 40-20-40 process, which focuses on understanding each partner’s perspective and fostering mutual understanding.

In addition to these techniques, we delve into other areas that may be affecting your communication, like unresolved emotions and personal challenges. As part of our comprehensive approach, we also offer a range of other therapy services to address these related issues.

The Warm and Inclusive Environment at Stickney Counseling - We strive to provide a warm, non-judgmental environment where you can feel comfortable discussing your concerns. We understand that discussing communication issues can be emotionally challenging, and we’re here to support you every step of the way.

Conclusion: The Long-Term Benefits of Addressing Communication Issues through Couples Therapy

Addressing communication issues through couples therapy has far-reaching benefits that extend beyond immediate conflict resolution. It cultivates a deeper understanding between partners, enhances emotional intimacy, and fosters a healthier relationship dynamic.

By investing in couples therapy, couples can effectively dismantle communication barriers, resulting in fewer misunderstandings and less frustration. They can also learn to express their feelings and needs more effectively, promoting mutual respect and understanding.

In the long-term, couples can experience improved relationship satisfaction and a renewed commitment to each other. Moreover, the skills learned in therapy, such as empathic listening and the use of “I” statements, can be applied to other interpersonal relationships, leading to overall improved communication skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is mental health therapy?

 

Mental health therapy is a professional service provided by trained therapists to help individuals address and manage their mental health concerns. It involves talking with a therapist in a safe and confidential environment to explore thoughts, feelings, behaviors, and develop strategies for improving well-being. It can help you deal with life's concerns and be a non bias place to process or it can be a place to heal new or old wounds.

What is the cost of therapy

 

The cost of therapy can vary. At Stickney Counseling, we would like to see everyone be able to get the care they need. Our Out of Pocket cost for services varies by therapist and type of service. The range for therapy is between $100 per session to $250 per session. We offer sliding scale for financial need.
