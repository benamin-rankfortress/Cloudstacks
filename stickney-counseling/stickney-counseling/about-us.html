About Us

Stickney Counseling

At Stickney Cousneling, we are dedicated to supporting your mental well-being and helping you navigate life’s challenges. We understand that seeking therapy can be a courageous step, and we commend you for taking that important first stride towards healing and personal growth. Our team of experienced and compassionate therapists is here to provide you with a safe and nurturing space where you can explore your thoughts, emotions, and life experiences. We believe in the power of therapy to facilitate positive change and empower individuals to lead more fulfilling lives. At our practice, we offer a range of therapeutic services tailored to meet your unique needs. Whether you’re struggling with anxiety, depression, relationship issues, trauma, or any other mental health concerns, we are here to listen, support, and guide you on your journey towards healing and transformation.
