About Us

Stickney Counseling

Our Therapy At Stickney Cousneling, we are dedicated to supporting your mental well-being and helping you navigate life’s challenges. We understand that seeking therapy can be a courageous step, and we commend you for taking that important first stride towards healing and personal growth. Our team of experienced and compassionate therapists is here to provide you with a safe and nurturing space where you can explore your thoughts, emotions, and life experiences. We believe in the power of therapy to facilitate positive change and empower individuals to lead more fulfilling lives. At our practice, we offer a range of therapeutic services tailored to meet your unique needs. Whether you’re struggling with anxiety, depression, relationship issues, trauma, or any other mental health concerns, we are here to listen, support, and guide you on your journey towards healing and transformation. We take a client-centered approach, which means that we value your individuality, experiences, and goals. Our therapists will work collaboratively with you to develop a personalized treatment plan that aligns with your needs and aspirations. We incorporate evidence-based therapeutic modalities and techniques to help you gain insight, build resilience, and develop effective coping strategies. We strive to create a warm, non-judgmental, and inclusive environment where you can feel heard, understood, and supported. We are committed to fostering a therapeutic relationship built on trust, empathy, and respect. We understand the importance of confidentiality, and your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. Rest assured that all your information will be handled with the highest level of discretion and adherence to professional ethical guidelines. Taking the first step towards therapy is an act of self-care and courage. We commend you for prioritizing your mental health and seeking the support you deserve. We are honored to be a part of your journey towards healing, growth, and a more fulfilling life. Please don’t hesitate to reach out to us with any questions or to schedule an appointment. We are here to support you every step of the way.
