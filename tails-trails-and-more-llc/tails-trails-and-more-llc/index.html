Frequently Asked Questions

Is it worth getting a trainer for a dog?

Training classes can help your dog learn some new skills, but there's more to it than that. It's also a great way to enrich your dog's daily life, build you into a more confident pet parent, and set both you and your four-legged friend up for success at home and in your community.

What are the ethics of dog trainers?

To always maintain professionalism through:

  • Providing your services honestly.
  • Treating animals and clients respectfully.
  • Valuing and preserving the privacy of clients.
  • Maintaining professionalism with colleagues and other professionals.
What is the quality of a good dog trainer?

Good trainers will be able to explain directions and answer their clients questions with kindness and positivity. Changing a dog's behavior takes time, energy, and a lot of hard work. The best trainers will spend as much time as it takes to get things right without getting frustrated with the dog or with the owner.
