About Us

Tails Trails and More LLC

"Tails Trails and More LLC, based in Denver's Goldsmith area, excels in dog training, focusing on obedience and behavioral needs. Our Denver dog training & board services cater to dogs of all temperaments. Our training, board & train options, including the famous 'sit means sit dog training,' are designed to meet diverse training needs. Experienced in leash obedience, we ensure your dog's behavior aligns with your lifestyle. Our programs, from dog training provides to high-energy exercises and non-anesthetic dental care, offer comprehensive solutions for your pet's needs. Contact Zack at Tails Trails & More Dog Training, Board & Train for obedience leash, aggressive dog, and other training programs right in your home."
