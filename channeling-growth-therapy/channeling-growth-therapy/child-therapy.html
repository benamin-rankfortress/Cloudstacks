Perfectionism Perfectionism is a trait that can often overlap with high-functioning anxiety and cause significant distress.

Holding oneself and others to extremely high standards and becoming consumed by details are common signs of perfectionism.

However, when taken too far, perfectionism can impair oneâ€™s ability to complete tasks or try new things due to the fear of making mistakes. Difficulty Relaxing For those with anxiety, relaxation is often very difficult. The mind races even during downtime, constantly turning to thoughts of unfinished tasks, unsolved problems, worries, or fears.

Quieting both the mind and body may seem impossible with the constant intrusion of anxious thoughts. Activities like meditation, deep breathing exercises, and yoga that require turning focus inward and achieving calm can be incredibly challenging for the anxious mind.

Little by little, the grip of anxiety loosens, and the mind and body learn to relax and unwind. Though difficult, relaxation is not impossible for those with anxiety.

Irritability Or Agitation Anxiety can often lead to extreme irritability or agitation, making even minor irritations seem disproportionately frustrating and provoking anger quickly. Restless energy may be present just below the surface, manifesting internally and directed outwardly. Frequent muscle tension caused by constant agitation can exacerbate irritability, making you more prone to lashing out.

It may feel like running on fumes, and taking steps to manage this agitation can help stabilize your mood. Engaging in activities such as exercise, deep breathing exercises, meditation, or counseling sessions with a mental health professional can help alleviate these symptoms.

Feeling Overwhelmed Easily One of the symptoms of high-functioning anxiety is feeling overwhelmed quickly. Even small commitments can add up and induce feelings of being overwhelmed.

Everyday cognitive tasks may require a lot of mental effort without proper treatment. Rather than beating yourself up for lack of focus, take note of how often you find yourself lost in anxious thoughts or exhausted by your own brain hyperactivity.

This can involve techniques such as mindfulness meditation or therapy sessions with a mental health professional. By addressing the root causes of your anxiety and finding effective coping mechanisms, you can improve your concentration and overall well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Can I Schedule a Consultation?

Scheduling a consultation with Channeling Growth Therapy is easy. Just click the “Book a Consultation” button on our website, and you’ll be guided through the steps to arrange your initial appointment. This session will allow you to discuss your concerns, clarify any doubts, and ascertain if our services are a suitable match for your needs.

What Is the Duration of a Telehealth Therapy Session?

A typical telehealth therapy session with Channeling Growth Therapy lasts about 50-60 minutes. However, we recognize the unique needs of each individual, and session lengths can be adjusted depending on your personal circumstances, the nature of your concerns, and the therapeutic approach undertaken.

Is My Privacy Protected in Channeling Growth Telehealth Therapy?

At Channeling Growth Therapy, we prioritize your privacy. We adhere strictly to all confidentiality standards and practices in the mental health sector. Our telehealth therapy platform complies with all privacy laws and regulations, including HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act). You can rest assured that your personal information, along with all your shared experiences during therapy sessions, are fully protected and kept confidential.

What Does Telehealth Therapy Entail?

Telehealth therapy, also referred to as online therapy, teletherapy, or e-therapy, is a mode of delivering mental health services remotely via secure, encrypted digital communication technology. At Channeling Growth Therapy, we leverage telehealth therapy by conducting sessions through video calls, providing the advantage of location flexibility and enabling you to engage in therapy from the convenience of your own space, wherever you may have internet connectivity.

Telehealth therapy offers a plethora of benefits, including accessibility for remote residents, saved commuting time, and the comfort and privacy of your chosen space. It serves as a breakthrough method to ensure therapeutic services are accessible to all, irrespective of geographical location.
