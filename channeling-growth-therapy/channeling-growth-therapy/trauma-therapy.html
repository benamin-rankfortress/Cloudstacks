Fees and Insurance

At Channeling Growth Therapy, we firmly believe in accessible mental health support, and this commitment is reflected in our flexible payment and insurance policy. We understand that financial considerations often play a crucial role in your decision to seek therapeutic services. That’s why we strive to ensure our services are not just of the highest quality but also financially accessible to you.

We are delighted to inform you that we are in-network with a variety of health insurance providers. This includes Beacon/Carelon Health Insurance, MHN Medi-Cal, and Cigna insurance. This means that if you are insured by any of these providers, your insurance plan may cover part or even the entire cost of the therapeutic services that we offer.

We encourage you to reach out to your insurance provider to verify your coverage for mental health services. They will provide detailed information on your deductible, copayments, and any out-of-pocket expenses that may be applicable.

In case you don’t have insurance or your plan doesn’t cover our services, please reach out to us. We are committed to working with you to ensure you receive the help you need. We can discuss our fees and explore payment plans to find an arrangement that works best for you.
