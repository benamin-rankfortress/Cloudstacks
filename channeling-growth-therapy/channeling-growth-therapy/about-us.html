About Us

Channeling Growth Therapy

We are a leading online telehealth group therapy practice dedicated to fostering mental health and wellness for all. Our team, skilled in addressing trauma, abuse, and providing age-specific therapy for children, teens, and adults, brings together a diverse array of expertise. We blend traditional therapeutic techniques with innovative practices to deliver personalized, high-quality care in a confidential and secure online setting. Our online sessions offer the flexibility and convenience necessary for today's busy lifestyles, making mental health services more accessible than ever. Our practice stands on a rich foundation of professional expertise and empathetic care, with a commitment to helping individuals navigate life's challenges.
