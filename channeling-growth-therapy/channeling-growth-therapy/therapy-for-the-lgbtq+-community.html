Frequently Asked Questions

How Frequently Should I Attend Telehealth Therapy?

 

The frequency of telehealth therapy sessions significantly varies based on your individual situation, therapeutic goals, and the treatment plan mutually agreed upon by you and your therapist. While some clients may benefit from weekly sessions, others might prefer bi-weekly or monthly sessions. The frequency of your therapy sessions will be collaboratively determined to ensure an optimal therapeutic process

How Can I Schedule a Consultation?

 

Scheduling a consultation with Channeling Growth Therapy is easy. Just click the “Book a Consultation” button on our website, and you’ll be guided through the steps to arrange your initial appointment. This session will allow you to discuss your concerns, clarify any doubts, and ascertain if our services are a suitable match for your needs.

What Can I Expect From a Telehealth Therapy Session?

 

Your telehealth therapy journey will be tailored to you. Each session is customized to address your specific needs and goals. Generally, a telehealth therapy session provides a secure, confidential, and judgment-free space for you to explore your thoughts, feelings, experiences, fears, and aspirations.

Our therapists provide active listening, offer professional guidance, and work collaboratively with you to establish effective strategies, exercises, and coping techniques to manage your challenges. Our core objective is to facilitate your journey of self-discovery, personal growth, healing, and resilience.

What Is the Duration of a Telehealth Therapy Session?

 

A typical telehealth therapy session with Channeling Growth Therapy lasts about 50-60 minutes. However, we recognize the unique needs of each individual, and session lengths can be adjusted depending on your personal circumstances, the nature of your concerns, and the therapeutic approach undertaken.

What If I Have Additional Questions About Telehealth Therapy?

 

We understand that commencing therapy, especially telehealth therapy, might prompt further questions or concerns. We’re here to address these queries and provide clarity. Don’t hesitate to contact us directly via our website, or during your consultation session, for any extra information you require. At Channeling Growth Therapy, we believe in transparent and open communication to ensure your comfort and trust throughout your telehealth therapy journey.

Remember, initiating change and personal growth often starts by reaching out. No question is too small, and no concern is insignificant when it comes to your mental health. We’re here to guide and support you on your journey towards improved mental health and personal development via telehealth therapy.
