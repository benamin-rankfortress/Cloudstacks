About Us

Core Recovery

Core Recovery is an outpatient treatment center that offers mental health, substance use, and eating disorder programs for adolescents and adults. At Core Recovery, we provide integrated care that consists of group therapy, individual therapy, psychiatric care, family systems therapy, and creative expression therapy. We believe in treating the mind and body in a manner that removes any shame or guilt around self-care. Core Recovery is here to help.
