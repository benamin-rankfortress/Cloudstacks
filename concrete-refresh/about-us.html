About Us

Concrete Refresh

Concrete Refresh is your local concrete coating company located in Milltown NJ and serving Middlesex County and surrounding areas. We offer epoxy flooring, quartz flooring, metallic epoxy floors, flake floors, garage remodels, urethane cement, and concrete overlays. We have many years of experience and enjoy serving both residential and commercial clients. If your looking for a trusted decorative concrete flooring solution, then were the company to call. We offer free estimates on al our projects. The best way to get in touch with us is to give us a call, but if you want to take a look at our past projects and fill out a contact form on our website, then feel free to do so. We look forward to serving you!
