About Us

Charleston Property Restoration

Charleston Property Restoration is a family owned business. We are homebuilders in Charleston WV! Aside from homebuilding we also work on property restoration, home remodeling, bathroom remodeling, kitchen remodeling and more! Kenny Webb is a skilled craftsman that does every job with diligence and attention to detail. He takes great pride in the work his company provides, and wants to be a blessing to his customers and his city by bringing new life to homes in the Greater Kanawha Valley. He trains his employees to the same standards by which he lives-being honest, reliable, and doing excellent work. We offer flexible scheduling and availability that suit your needs. Talk with us and see how we take your ideas and turn them to reality!
