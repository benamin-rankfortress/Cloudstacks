Frequently Asked Questions

What is a roll-off dumpster?

A roll-off dumpster is a type of dumpster that is typically used for construction or large-scale cleanout projects. These dumpsters are usually much larger than traditional residential or commercial ones, ranging in size from 10 to 40 yards.

How long can I keep a roll-off dumpster on my property?

Most roll-off dumpster rental companies will allow you to keep the dumpster on your property for anywhere from one week to one month. However, some companies may charge additional fees for extended rentals, so it is always best to check with the rental company beforehand.

Do I need a permit to rent a roll-off dumpster?

In most cases, you will not need a permit to rent a roll-off dumpster. However, there are some exceptions, such as if you plan to place the dumpster on a public street or if the dumpster will be larger than a specific size. Check with the rental company beforehand to find out if you need a permit.
