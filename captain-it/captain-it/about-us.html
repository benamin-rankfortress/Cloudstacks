About Us

Captain IT

Captain IT is a local IT support firm that specializes in providing "Instant Peace of Mind IT Services" to businesses in the Riverside and San Bernardino counties. Our core services include flat-fee Managed IT Services, Data Backup & Recovery, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, and VoIP Phone Systems. Captain IT is a company that was born from the identified need for reliable and quality services in the IT services marketplace. Our principles are hard work, helping others, tenacity, and commitment to excellence. Locations served include Riverside, Corona, San Bernardino, Fontana, Rancho Cucamonga, Pomona, Claremont, Ontario, Chino, Rialto, Temecula, Claremont, Lake Elsinore, Los Angeles, and Orage County.
