About Us

Creative Healing Mental Health Center

Creative Healing Mental Health Center, located in Lake Hallie, WI, provides mental health therapy services. We welcome you and the beauty in the way you experience life. You may be here seeking answers to the feelings of disconnections, fear, overwhelm, irritability or overall feeling that something is wrong with you that prevents you from living the life you envisioned. Mental Health therapy can help heal these experiences live presently. Specializing in EMDR therapy along with mindfulness to release the burdens you may be carrying to feel lighter and confident in yourself. Diagnosis specialities include creating a sense of connection within trauma, PTSD, schizophrenia, dissociation, mood disorders, depression and anxiety diagnosis.
