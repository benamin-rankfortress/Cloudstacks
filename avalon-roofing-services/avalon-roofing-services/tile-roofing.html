Frequently Asked Questions

Whats the cost of a new asphalt shingle roof in Manteca?

Shingles themselves are affordable, averaging about $.80 to $1.20 per square foot. Because asphalt shingles are easy to install, it will also keep labor costs low. The final cost of your new asphalt shingle roof will depend on a few factors such as the pitch of the roof, square footage of the roof, and other features.

What are the installation requirements for wood shake roofing?

The installation requirements for wood shake roofing vary depending on your location. However, most roofs require a minimum of six inches of insulation, and you will need to hire a qualified contractor to install your shake roof.

How do I know when my shingles need to be replaced?

If you notice any of the following issues, you should consult an experienced Manteca roofer about replacing your asphalt shingles:

 

  • Curled or buckled shingles
  • Missing shingles
  • Cracked shingles
  • Lifted shingles
  • Ceramic granules accumulating in your gutters and downspouts
