About Us

Avalon Roofing Services

My name is Jerry Garcia and I am the owner of Avalon Roofing Services. I began my career in the roofing trade in 1993. I started off as a "grunt" which is just a general laborer. Slowly but surely, I worked my way up to Journeyman roofer, then to Supervisor, then an Estimator & Project Manager for a multi-million dollar roofing company. In my almost 30-year career, I've learned many things. But I think the most important thing I've learned is this. Being a successful roofing company doesn't just consist of giving the customer a nice-looking roof. What I believe is just as important, is building the highest level of TRUST possible with the customer! That means making sure the customers know that our number one priority is them.
