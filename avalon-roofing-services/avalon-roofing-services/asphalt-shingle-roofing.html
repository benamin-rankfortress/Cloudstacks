Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right type of wood shake roofing?

Choosing the right type of wood shake roofing can be tricky, but it is important to consider your personal preferences, as well as the climate where you live. If you live in a hot climate, you may want to choose a type of wood shake roofing that is resistant to heat and fire. If you live in a cold climate, you may want to choose a type of wood shake roofing that is resistant to moisture and ice.

What are the risks and benefits of wood shake roofing?

Wood shake roofing is a very durable type of roofing, but it does have some risks associated with it. One of the biggest risks is that wood shake roofs are susceptible to fire, so you will need to take precautions to protect your home from fire. However, the benefits of wood shake roofing far outweigh the risks, and it is a popular roofing choice for many homeowners.

What are the different types of wood shake roofing?

There are a variety of different types of wood shake roofing available, and each one has its own unique look and feel. Some of the most popular types of wood shake roofing include cedar, pine, and aspen.
