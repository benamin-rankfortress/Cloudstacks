Lake Travis Lighted Boat Parade

The Lake Travis Lighted Boat Parade is a festive event that takes place in December on Lake Travis in Austin, Texas, typically during the holiday season. Boaters decorate their boats with colorful lights and decorations, creating a dazzling display on the water. The Lake Travis light parade is a popular and beloved tradition in the area, drawing locals and visitors alike to enjoy the spectacle. It’s a great way to get into the holiday spirit and enjoy the beauty of Lake Travis at night.
