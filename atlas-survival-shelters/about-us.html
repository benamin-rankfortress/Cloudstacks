About Us

Atlas Survival Shelters

"Our world headquarters is located just an hour east of Dallas, Texas. This plant manufactures shelters with sizes ranging from the affordable BombNado that goes in under new homes being constructed to the popular round culvert pipes which have been around for decades to the Billionaire class poured in place concrete hardened bunkers that cost into the millions. We have engineers and a staff of project managers that travel the globe installing and supervising projects from small residential to large military projects."
