About Us

Momentum Psychology, PLLC

Momentum Psychology is a specialty therapy practice focusing on the needs of high-achieving executives, entrepreneurs, lawyers, physicians, creatives, athletes, and other high performers in Charlotte, NC and throughout NC and the Southeast in places like Raleigh-Durham, NC, Nashville, TN, Atlanta, GA, DC and others. Since 2019, we have provided therapy for stress, burnout, anxiety, trauma, depression, habit change, parenting, and relationship and work problems. We are all doctoral-level psychologists and trained in evidence-based treatments like CBT, ACT, DBT, and exposure and response prevention. We are committed to helping our clients THRIVE not just survive!
