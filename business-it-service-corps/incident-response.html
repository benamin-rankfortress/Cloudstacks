Understanding Managed Service Providers (MSP) - Managed Service Providers, often referred to as MSPs, are third-party companies that manage a business’s IT infrastructure. They essentially act as an external IT department. But unlike traditional IT staff, MSPs provide a broader range of services, from network security and data backup to system monitoring and cloud services.

How Do MSPs Operate? - Most MSPs operate under a subscription-based model. Businesses pay a regular fee for their services, which typically include 24/7 monitoring, maintenance, and troubleshooting. It’s like having a guardian angel constantly watching over your IT environment, ready to swoop in and rectify any issues before they cause serious damage.
