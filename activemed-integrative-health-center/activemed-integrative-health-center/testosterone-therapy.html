Overview Of ActiveMed Integrative Health Center

Located in the picturesque locales of Encinitas, Poway, and San Diego, ActiveMed Integrative Health Center is a beacon of holistic healthcare. The center’s mission is to provide the highest quality of compassionate and effective healthcare by blending proven medical treatments with traditional alternative therapies. The center is led by an experienced and multidisciplinary team, including Matt Alavi, L.Ac., Dipl. OM, and a host of doctors, acupuncturists, and massage therapists.

Services Offered At ActiveMed Integrative Health Center

ActiveMed is dedicated to providing natural medical services to assist you with your health and wellness goals. The center offers a variety of therapies that have been proven effective and have been used for thousands of years.
