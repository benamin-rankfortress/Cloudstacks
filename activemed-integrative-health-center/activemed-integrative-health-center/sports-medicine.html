Definition Of Integrative Health

Integrative health is a revolutionary concept that combines conventional and complementary practices to treat the whole person. By utilizing all appropriate therapeutic approaches, the focus is on the patient’s health, the therapeutic relationship, and the individual as a whole. It is not merely a method of treatment, but a way of life that emphasizes prevention, patient empowerment, and a harmonious balance between the body, mind, and spirit.

The Importance Of Integrative Health

The importance of integrative health lies in its comprehensive approach to wellness. Instead of simply treating ailments, it investigates the underlying causes. It understands that our physical, mental, and emotional health are intertwined, and that a balanced lifestyle is key to optimal health.
