I Want a Website But I'm Not Sure Where to Start.

"Don’t worry, lots of people feel a little overwhelmed and confused and aren’t sure how to get started. It’s easier than starting with a minimum and adding to it. Once you have your notes put together you should have a pretty good idea of how to describe what you’re looking for when you talk to a web designer."
