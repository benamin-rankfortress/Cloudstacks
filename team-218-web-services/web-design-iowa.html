Understanding Artificial Intelligence Basics - So what exactly is AI anyway? Essentially, it’s about machines mimicking human intelligence – things like learning, understanding language, recognizing images, making decisions. But AI goes way beyond that – it’s transforming major industries like healthcare, finance, transportation. And a key superpower of AI systems is the ability to learn and adapt on their own, without needing humans to spell everything out for them upfront in code.

Alright, so we’ve covered the basic definition of artificial intelligence. But how does this amazing technology actually work its magic? What’s under the hood?

Let’s trace the journey of an AI system and see how it mimics human-level intelligence. First, we need to feed data into these AI machines. AI systems scrape the web for data – lots of data, from images to text documents to numbers. Think of this as the food that fuels AI. Once fed, AI systems preprocess and organize this data so they can analyze it.

Here’s where the special sauce comes in – the algorithms. They’re kind of like a secret recipe guiding how the AI will learn patterns from data and make decisions. The most advanced algorithms powering AI today are called neural networks, modeled after the human brain. I know, mind blown!

Based on its algorithms, the AI creates models by detecting patterns and relationships in large volumes of data it has processed. This is considered “training” the machine. Once trained well, voila! We have an intelligent model that can now independently take in new data like images, speech, and text, analyze them, and take actions or make predictions.

So in a nutshell, that’s the magic process bringing AI to life! We feed data to algorithms which train learning models to mimic specialized human intelligence skills like seeing, listening, reading, deciding. Then AI feels ready to flex its skills in the real world. Of course, it takes a whole lot of complex math and computing power too! But no need to sweat the technical details – I wanted you to just get an inside look at how AI systems “learn” and “think” in a human-like fashion. The rest is abstract computational wizardry!

So in a nutshell, that’s what happens under the AI brain’s hood! We give machines large volumes of data, set algorithms loose on that data to uncover hidden structures, obtain learnings through model building, and enable those learned models to handle new real world data and situations based on their programmed intelligence. The crucial process of machine learning gives AI systems this magical ability to learn, reason and make decisions independently based on their data driven experiences – bringing theory into practical tangible capabilities through code! Keep in mind that AI doesn’t actually create anything – it just rewords, reformats, repurposes existing information and regurgitates it.

I Want a Website But I'm Not Sure Where to Start.

"Don’t worry, lots of people feel a little overwhelmed and confused and aren’t sure how to get started. We recommend that you take some time to browse the internet to look at some websites in your business niche. While you’re doing this, make notes of the URL (web address) for sites you like and then include any specifics about what you like. Make note of colors, layout, features and other specifics things you like. Next, think about what you want from your website. I often tell people to imagine the utopia website where you could have anything you want. Make notes of any bells and whistles you want. Then you can work backwards from there if needed, eliminating things that might be too expensive or realistically don’t make sense. Always start with anything you can think of and go back from there. It’s easier than starting with a minimum and adding to it. Once you have your notes put together you should have a pretty good idea of how to describe what you’re looking for when you talk to a web designer."
