Understanding Insurance Coverage For Acupuncture - The question, “Does my health insurance cover acupuncture treatments?” is one that we often hear from our patients. It’s important to understand that insurance coverage for acupuncture varies greatly depending on the specific insurance plan.

Many Medicare Advantage plans are now incorporating complementary therapies, including acupuncture, into their coverage options. According to a study on Medicare Advantage plans, these plans increasingly recognize the importance of holistic healthcare and include acupuncture among their covered services. However, coverage may come with certain limitations and restrictions, such as preauthorization requirements, network provider limitations, and visit limits.

We recommend checking with your insurance provider to understand your coverage for acupuncture services. At ActiveMed, our staff will also assist you in navigating through the complexities of insurance coverage.

Other Payment Options For Acupuncture Services - We believe that everyone should have access to the health benefits of acupuncture, regardless of their insurance coverage. To ensure this, we offer alternative payment options to cater to our patients’ diverse financial needs.

For patients without insurance coverage for acupuncture or those preferring to pay out of pocket, we provide competitive pricing for our services. We’re committed to ensuring that our holistic healthcare solutions are accessible and affordable.
