Questions to Ask When Choosing an ABA Provider

When seeking ‘ABA therapy near me’, it’s crucial to make an informed decision about your child’s provider. The right provider will be a cornerstone in your child’s development journey. To help you make the best choice, here are some key questions to ask potential ABA therapy providers.

Staff Qualifications and Training

First things first, ask about the qualifications of the staff. Specifically, inquire about the number of Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) on staff. These professionals are experts in ABA therapy and their presence is a strong indicator of a quality provider. Also, ask about the training that therapists receive and the frequency of this training. At AWC Behavioral Health, we take pride in our highly trained team and their commitment to ongoing professional development.

ABA therapy

Session Details and Goal-Setting Process

Understanding what a typical ABA session looks like is vital. This includes the setting (home-based or clinic-based), duration, and structure of the sessions. Also, ask about how goals for your child are determined and if they consider input from parents. At AWC, we believe in a collaborative approach where the family plays a significant role in goal setting.

Safety Measures and Policies - Safety should be a top priority for any ABA provider. Ask how they manage safety concerns during therapy sessions. We at AWC Behavioral Health prioritize your child’s safety and wellbeing, ensuring that all our sessions take place in a secure and empathetic setting.
