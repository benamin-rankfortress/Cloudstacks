Frequently Asked Questions

Which rims fit my vehicle?

Not every rim fits every car. The correct size of a rim depends on several factors. Before purchasing a rim set, it is important to pay attention to the rim size, bolt holes, pitch size, hub diameter, ET value and J size. Don't feel like looking this up? You will automatically find suitable wheels for your car via our wheel configurator/license plate finder .

What are Breedset wheels?

A large number of cars can also be equipped with a so-called “wideset”, or a “staggered” set of rims. This means that the rear rims are wider than the front, and therefore a wider tire must also be mounted at the rear. This is usually installed on cars with rear-wheel drive, but sometimes also on cars with 4-wheel drive. View wide sets

Can I trade in my old rims?

It is often possible to trade in your old rims at wheelpoint. We will check your rims and make an offer. read more

Can I order online and have rims & tires fitted?

We can fit your tires and/or rims to your car in our workshop in Uden. If you choose to have the order delivered to your home, you must place the tires and/or rims on the car yourself. Naturally, the order will be delivered completely ready for installation. Would you like to install the rims yourself? View our manual.
