We utilize a variety of therapeutic techniques, including Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT), which helps couples exit painful relational cycles and create new healing patterns of interaction. This approach is designed to navigate conflict successfully and, most importantly, deepen intimacy and connection with your partner.

The Team of Experienced Therapists - Our team consists of highly skilled counselors who specialize in relationship dynamics. Understanding the Purpose of Marriage Counseling: Marriage counseling — often referred to as couples therapy or marriage therapy — is a professional service designed to help couples identify, address, and resolve their relationship problems. The core purpose of marriage counseling is to improve communication, deepen emotional connection, enhance conflict resolution skills, and equip couples with practical solutions. Engaging with the process can significantly improve the quality of a marriage, whether you’re in the thrall of a major dispute or seeking to enhance an already warm and loving relationship.

The Importance of Seeking Professional Help - Choosing the right marriage counseling can be an intimidating prospect when you’re already facing the emotional turbulence of a relationship in difficulty. However, the intervention of a licensed and experienced marriage counselor can provide a fresh perspective and expert guidance to help navigate these complex issues.

Here is a quick checklist to understand the benefits of seeking marriage counseling: Improved communication skills, Effective conflict resolution strategies, Enhancement of emotional and physical intimacy, Learning healthier relationship patterns, Avoiding divorce by resolving underlying issues, Seeking professional help can also redirect the trajectory of a declining relationship towards a healthier and happier future.

Conclusion: The Role of Commitment and Patience in the Success of Marriage Counseling. In the realm of marriage counseling, success isn’t measured by speed, but rather by the sustainability of progress. Change takes time, and improving a relationship is no different. As echoed in our counseling for adolescents, understanding that setbacks are a natural part of growth can help maintain commitment to the counseling journey.

Patience is key, as is commitment. In fact, research suggests that the success rate of marriage counseling is around 70% according to the Journal of Marital and Family Therapy. This success is largely dependent on the couple’s commitment to work on their relationship. In our experience, we’ve seen that couples who are committed to the process of therapy, even if one partner was initially reluctant, often experience significant improvements in their relationships.

Frequently Asked Questions

I am interested in telehealth; how do I determine if it is an option for me?

 

We encourage you to discuss your interest in telehealth with your clinician during your next appointment to determine if this might be a fit for you. Unless you are linked to a Telehealth exclusive site, currently telehealth is offered only for follow-up appointments, not first-time patients. If you do not currently have an appointment scheduled, please contact us.

What if my provider doesn’t offer telehealth?

 

If you do not see your provider listed as an available Telehealth participant, please to find out more information.

Will my insurance cover telehealth visits?

 

Most commercial insurances do cover telehealth visits, but coverage can vary by insurance carrier. We strongly recommend that you contact your insurance carrier prior to scheduling a telehealth visit, to inquire if telehealth is indeed a covered benefit for your plan.

Are telehealth video appointments HIPAA Complaint?

 

Peace of Mind Wellness and Family Counseling, Inc. ensures the interaction between the patient and clinician is completely secure and HIPAA compliant. The teleconferencing software encrypts all forms of data, including video and audio.

How To Cancel My Appointment

 

Please call your location to cancel an appointment or select “Cancel” on the appointment reminder you receive to your email. This must be done at least 48 hours in advance or your cancellation can not be received.
