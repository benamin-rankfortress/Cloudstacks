Couples Counseling

Solution-Focused Therapy - Solution-Focused Therapy is a goal-oriented approach that helps couples find solutions to specific problems or disputes. We work with couples to set and work towards relationship goals. This method can be particularly helpful when dealing with issues like communication breakdowns or recurring conflicts.

Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) - EFT involves discussing specific distressing events in the relationship. These discussions can help you understand the emotions and behaviors behind what occurred. Its a useful approach for couples looking to rebuild their emotional bond and navigate through feelings of betrayal, anger, or fear.

Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT) - CBT is often used to treat issues like anxiety and depression, but its also an effective form of marriage counseling. It can address difficulties with communication and conflict resolution by helping couples identify and change negative behavior and communication patterns.

We at Peace of Mind Wellness & Family Counseling are experienced in these and other therapeutic approaches. Based on your unique circumstances and goals, we will work together to choose the most suitable approach for your marriage counseling journey.

The success of any form of marriage counseling is largely dependent on the commitment and participation of both partners. Its about working together to find solutions, improve your relationship, and create a happier, healthier future together.

Evaluating the Effectiveness of Marriage Counseling. One of the most common questions we receive at Peace of Mind Wellness & Family Counseling is Does marriage counseling really work? The answer is a resounding yes. The effectiveness of marriage counseling can be evaluated on various levels, including improved communication and conflict resolution skills, enhanced emotional and physical health, and the rebuilding of the bond between partners.

Improvement in Communication and Conflict Resolution Skills. The first noticeable change is typically in the couples communication and conflict resolution skills. Many couples struggle with these aspects, often leading to frequent conflicts and misunderstandings. Through marriage counseling, couples learn to express their feelings, needs, and concerns more effectively, fostering better understanding and empathy.

Counseling can also equip couples with practical conflict resolution strategies, helping them handle disagreements in a healthier way. With the guidance of our experienced therapists, you can move away from destructive patterns of communication and towards a positive, constructive dialogue that strengthens your relationship.

Enhanced Emotional and Physical Health - Next, marriage counseling can lead to significant improvements in both emotional and physical health. Studies by the American Association of Marriage and Family Therapy (AAMFT) report that 90% of couples who try marriage counseling say their emotional health has improved. Interestingly, two-thirds also report improvements in their physical health. This shows that the benefits of counseling extend beyond your relationship and well into your overall well-being.

Rebuilding the Bond Between Partners - Lastly, but most importantly, marriage counseling is instrumental in rebuilding the bond between partners. This process may involve healing past hurts, rebuilding trust, and reigniting the emotional connection that may have faded over time. Our therapists are trained in various therapy approaches, including Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), which are effective in addressing these issues directly.

As a result, couples often report a higher level of relationship satisfaction after seeking professional help. They not only overcome their immediate relationship issues but also strengthen their bond and enhance their ability to navigate future challenges together.

In conclusion, evaluating the effectiveness of marriage counseling involves a holistic look at the improvements in your communication, health, and emotional bond. The journey may not always be easy, but with commitment and patience, the results can be profoundly rewarding. Rest assured, we at Peace of Mind Wellness & Family Counseling are here to support you every step of the way.

Individual Counseling

How to Choose the Right Marriage Counselor - Choosing the right marriage counselor is a crucial step in your journey towards a healthier and happier relationship. Heres a simple checklist to guide you through the process.

Checking Qualifications and Experience - Start by confirming the counselors educational background and licensure. They should preferably hold a masters or doctoral degree in psychology, counseling, or a related field. Also, dont shy away from asking about their experience in couples counseling. A counselor with a substantial amount of experience will likely have a profound understanding of relationship dynamics and effective therapeutic strategies.

Considering the Therapists Approach - Different therapists may adopt varying therapeutic approaches. Some may use a solution-focused method, while others may prioritize emotion-focused therapy. Its essential to understand the counselors approach to ensure it aligns with your comfort level and therapy goals.

Assessing the Comfort Level with the Therapist - Feeling comfortable and understood by your counselor is crucial for effective counseling. Arrange a preliminary consultation or a brief meeting with the counselor to gauge their communication style, empathy, and overall compatibility. Dont be afraid to shop around until you find a marriage counselor who meets your expectations and makes you feel at ease.

Evaluating the Cost and Accessibility - Marriage counseling costs can vary depending on the therapist and their level of experience. Its important to discuss the cost upfront and ensure it fits within your budget. Also, consider the accessibility of the counselor. If you have a busy schedule, you might prefer a counselor who offers flexible appointment times or online sessions.

Exploring Online Therapy Options - Online therapy is an excellent alternative to traditional face-to-face counseling. It provides flexible scheduling and can be accessed from the comfort of your home. At Peace of Mind Wellness & Family Counseling, we offer online couples therapy with licensed and highly qualified therapists who have extensive experience in marriage counseling.

Choosing the right marriage counselor is not a decision to be made lightly. It requires careful consideration of various factors, including qualifications, experience, approach, cost, and compatibility. The ultimate goal is to find a counselor who can provide the support and guidance you need to strengthen and improve your relationship.

Preparing for Your First Marriage Counseling Session. After making the important decision to seek marriage counseling and choosing the right counselor, the next step is preparing for your first session. This is a crucial step in the process and can set the tone for your counseling journey. Here are a few key areas to focus on.

Being Open and Honest - Honesty is the cornerstone of effective therapy. As you approach your first session, its crucial to be open and honest with your counselor and your partner. This includes being honest about your feelings, your concerns, and your expectations for therapy.

I am interested in telehealth; how do I determine if it is an option for me?

 

We encourage you to discuss your interest in telehealth with your clinician during your next appointment to determine if this might be a fit for you. Unless you are linked to a Telehealth exclusive site, currently telehealth is offered only for follow-up appointments, not first-time patients. If you do not currently have an appointment scheduled, please contact us.

What if my provider doesnt offer telehealth?

 

If you do not see your provider listed as an available Telehealth participant, please to find out more information.

Will my insurance cover telehealth visits?

 

Most commercial insurances do cover telehealth visits, but coverage can vary by insurance carrier. We strongly recommend that you contact your insurance carrier prior to scheduling a telehealth visit, to inquire if telehealth is indeed a covered benefit for your plan.

Are telehealth video appointments HIPAA Complaint?

 

Peace of Mind Wellness and Family Counseling, Inc. ensures the interaction between the patient and clinician is completely secure and HIPAA compliant. The teleconferencing software encrypts all forms of data, including video and audio.

How To Cancel My Appointment

 

Please call your location to cancel an appointment or select “Cancel” on the appointment reminder you receive to your email. This must be done at least 48 hours in advance or your cancellation can not be received.
