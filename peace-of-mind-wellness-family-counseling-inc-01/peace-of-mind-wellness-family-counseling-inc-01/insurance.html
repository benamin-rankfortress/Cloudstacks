Attachment, Differentiation, and Mindfulness-Based Somatic Depth Therapy. Another approach we use in our practice is a combination of attachment theory, differentiation, and mindfulness-based somatic depth therapy. This comprehensive approach helps individuals understand their deep-seated feelings and how these impact their relationships.

Attachment theory helps couples understand their emotional bonding and how their past experiences influence their current relationship dynamics. Differentiation, on the other hand, encourages individuals to develop a strong sense of self while maintaining closeness with their partner. Mindfulness-based somatic depth therapy, meanwhile, helps individuals focus on their physical and emotional experiences, promoting a greater sense of self-awareness and understanding.

Other Notable Counseling Approaches - In addition to the Gottman Method and attachment-based therapy, other counseling techniques may be used depending on the couples specific needs and goals. These may include solution-focused therapy, cognitive-behavioral therapy, experiential therapy, and imago therapy. Each of these approaches brings unique strengths and benefits, and our therapists are trained to select and apply the most appropriate techniques for each couple.

For example, solution-focused therapy is used to help couples identify and work towards specific relationship goals. Cognitive-behavioral therapy, on the other hand, is used to help couples identify and change negative behavior and communication patterns.

In the end, our ultimate goal is to provide couples counseling that is tailored to the unique needs of each couple. By using a blend of these effective therapeutic approaches, we aim to help couples build stronger, healthier, and more fulfilling relationships.

How to Prepare for Couples Counseling: Setting Goals and Expectations. Before embarking on the journey of couples counseling, lay some groundwork. This involves reflecting on your relationship challenges and articulating your goals for counseling. This preparation can help make your counseling sessions more productive and focused.

Reflecting on Your Relationship Challenges - The first step in preparing for couples counseling is to reflect on the challenges in your relationship. This involves taking a step back and objectively assessing the issues youre facing as a couple. Is it a recurring argument about household chores? Or perhaps deeper issues such as differing financial values, resentment, or resistance to change?

Its crucial to remember that the goal of this reflection isnt to assign blame, but to identify the patterns causing tension in your relationship. Identifying these challenges can help your couples counselor better understand your situation and provide more targeted assistance.

Articulating Your Goals for Couples Counseling - Once youve identified your relationship challenges, the next step is to articulate your goals for couples counseling. What do you hope to achieve through the counseling process? Maybe you want to improve your communication skills, deepen your understanding of each other, or find ways to resolve conflicts more effectively.

When setting your goals, its important to be realistic and patient. Changes wont happen overnight, but with commitment and consistency, progress can be made. The aim isnt to fix your partner or make your relationship perfectits to develop healthier dynamics and find shared understanding.

What matters most is how you handle these situations, and couples counseling can be a great tool to help you do so.

Frequently Asked Questions

I am interested in telehealth; how do I determine if it is an option for me?

 

We encourage you to discuss your interest in telehealth with your clinician during your next appointment to determine if this might be a fit for you. Unless you are linked to a Telehealth exclusive site, currently telehealth is offered only for follow-up appointments, not first-time patients. If you do not currently have an appointment scheduled, please contact us.

What if my provider doesnt offer telehealth?

 

If you do not see your provider listed as an available Telehealth participant, please to find out more information.

Will my insurance cover telehealth visits?

 

Most commercial insurances do cover telehealth visits, but coverage can vary by insurance carrier. We strongly recommend that you contact your insurance carrier prior to scheduling a telehealth visit, to inquire if telehealth is indeed a covered benefit for your plan.

Are telehealth video appointments HIPAA Complaint?

 

Peace of Mind Wellness and Family Counseling, Inc. ensures the interaction between the patient and clinician is completely secure and HIPAA compliant. The teleconferencing software encrypts all forms of data, including video and audio.

How To Cancel My Appointment

 

Please call your location to cancel an appointment or select “Cancel” on the appointment reminder you receive to your email. This must be done at least 48 hours in advance or your cancellation can not be received.
