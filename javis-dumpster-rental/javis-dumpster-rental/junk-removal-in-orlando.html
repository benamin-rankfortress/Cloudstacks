Frequently Asked Questions

What are the unacceptable materials for roll-off dumpsters?

Generally speaking, you cannot dispose of hazardous wastes in your dumpster. Below is a list of hazardous wastes that are unacceptable materials to put into a roll off dumpster. Please contact your Javi’s Dumpster Rental about any items you are uncertain about.
PCB’s
Lead
Acid Batteries (automotive batteries, etc.),
Cadmium containing batteries
Used oil, Oil filters or Oil containers
White goods: refrigerators, freezers, ranges, water heaters, washers, dryers, etc.
Whole tires
Liquids or Sludge
Street sweepings
Regulated Asbestos (friable)
Mercury containing lamps and devices (fluorescent light tubes, ballast, thermostats, smoke detectors)
Propane tanks
Refrigerant parts
Automotive parts
Railroad ties, Telephone poles
Electronics, Computers, TVs, Stereos, etc.

Is it necessary for someone to be present when they deliver my dumpster?

It is not necessary for someone to be present when your dumpster is delivered. However, if you have any special directions or placement requests, it is advisable that someone is present to be sure that the driver understands your needs.

How long can I keep the dumpster?

You can keep the dumpster for the entire length of the contract. Typical rental periods are 7-14 days. However, we are very service oriented and we have the flexibility to work with you if it looks like your project may last longer than the agreed upon contract period. Please be aware that additional rental fees may be added to your original invoice. So please call us before the end of the rental agreement and our sales staff will work with you.

How far in advance do I need to order a dumpster rental?

Please order your dumpster 48 hours in advance if possible. However, in certain situations, your Dumpster Rental representative may be able to deliver the container sooner.
