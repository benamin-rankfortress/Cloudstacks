Frequently Asked Questions

What are my payment options?

We think you’ll be happy with the dumpster rental price you get from Dumpster Rental. You can pay for your dumpster rental in advance with PayPal, Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover.

How far in advance do I need to order a dumpster rental?

Please order your dumpster 48 hours in advance if possible. However, in certain situations, your Dumpster Rental representative may be able to deliver the container sooner.

Will the dumpster damage my driveway or lawn?

Due to the dumpster’s weight, there is a risk that it may cause some damage to your lawn and certain types of driveways. If this is a concern of yours, we recommend having pieces of plywood ready when the container is delivered to place under the wheels of the dumpster. This should help minimize any damage. Javi’s Dumpster Rentals does not take any responsibility or liability for any damage caused to your lawn or driveway, however.
