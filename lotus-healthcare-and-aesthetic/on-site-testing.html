Systemic hormone therapy takes a broad approach. These medications can come as pills, patches, gels, emulsions, or even sprays, and they work throughout the entire body. They’re particularly effective for widespread symptoms like hot flashes, night sweats, and for preventing osteoporosis. Since they circulate in the bloodstream, they can provide relief in multiple areas of the body.

Low-Dose Vaginal Products

For those mainly troubled by vaginal symptoms — think dryness, itching, or discomfort during intercourse — low-dose vaginal products might be the answer. They come in creams, tablets, or rings and focus on delivering relief right where it’s needed, with minimal absorption into the rest of the body. This localized approach minimizes systemic effects, making it a suitable option for women who need to target specific areas.

Benefits Of Hormone Replacement Therapy

Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT) offers several benefits, particularly for individuals navigating the challenging waters of menopause. At Lotus Healthcare and Aesthetics, we’ve seen firsthand how HRT can transform lives. Let’s break down the key benefits.

Relief From Menopausal Symptoms

One of the most immediate and tangible benefits of hormone replacement therapy is the relief from menopausal symptoms. These symptoms can range from mild to debilitating and include hot flashes, night sweats, mood swings, and vaginal discomfort. Our expert, Lindsay Hetrick, emphasizes that systemic estrogen therapy remains the most effective treatment for these symptoms. It’s not just about easing discomfort; it’s about restoring your quality of life during and after the menopausal transition.

Prevention Of Osteoporosis And Fractures

Menopause can lead to a decrease in bone density, increasing the risk of osteoporosis and fractures. This is where HRT steps in as a preventive measure. By replacing estrogen that your body stops producing at menopause, HRT can help maintain bone density and reduce the risk of fractures. This benefit is significant, as it addresses a long-term health risk associated with menopause, providing not just immediate relief but also future health security.

Improved Sleep And Quality Of Life

Healthcare

The impact of menopause on sleep patterns and overall quality of life cannot be overstated. Many individuals experience sleep disturbances, which in turn affect their daily functioning and well-being. Hormone replacement therapy has been shown to improve sleep quality by reducing the frequency of night sweats and hot flashes. By addressing these symptoms, HRT can significantly improve your quality of life, leading to better mental health, increased energy levels, and a more positive outlook on life.

How does the weight loss program work?

Our weight loss clinic functions as a specialized center that offers tailored programs and support to help individuals achieve their weight loss goals. Here’s the breakdown of how the weight loss clinic generally operates:

  1. Initial Assessment: You’ll undergo an initial evaluation where healthcare professionals assess your current health status, weight, medical history, lifestyle, and goals. This assessment helps in designing a personalized weight loss plan.
  2. Customized Plan: Based on your assessment, the clinic will create a personalized weight loss plan that may include a combination of dietary guidelines, exercise routines, behavioral therapy, and usually prescribe medications or supplements. We will work with your insurance company to prescribe medications which are covered by your policy. However, in the event your insurance does not cover the cost of medications, we have available medication in our office for purchase such as Semaglutide and Tirzepatide.
  3. Regular Monitoring: You’ll have regular check-ins and monitoring to track progress, adjust the plan if necessary, and provide support and guidance. This involves scheduled office visits typically every 4 weeks, either telehealth (virtual) appointments, in person visits or a mix of both.
  4. Education and Support: Our providers and team provide education on nutrition, portion control, meal planning, and behavioral strategies to help you make sustainable lifestyle changes. They might also offer counseling or support groups for motivation and accountability.
  5. Medical Supervision: Our clinic provides access to medical professionals who oversee your progress and address any health concerns or challenges that arise during your weight loss journey.
  6. Long-term Maintenance: After achieving your weight loss goals, the clinic provides guidance on how to maintain your weight in the long term, offering strategies to prevent weight regain and sustain a healthy lifestyle.
