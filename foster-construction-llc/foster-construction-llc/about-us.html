About Us

Foster Construction, LLC

Foster Construction is a locally owned and operated small business founded by Brett Hodgdon, a professional engineer, and Amber Hodgdon, a former corporate lawyer. From the work we do to the relationships we build, we take great pride in upholding our commitment to provide outstanding customer service, consistent results and lasting value to our customers. No matter how big or small, we see every project as an opportunity to live up to the values that have shaped who we are as a business today: Quality, Commitment, Teamwork, Innovation and Leadership.
