Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take insurance?

We are in-network providers with Cigna, Magellan, Tricare, Aetna, AvMed, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Optum and United Health networks currently.

Otherwise, all costs are self-pay. Please note: if you have insurance, while we go to significant lengths to ensure our estimates are as accurate as possible, please be aware that everything is an estimate until the claim has been finalized. Our patients are responsible for knowing health benefits covered by their insurance plan.

All copays are due at the time of service and are collected through the credit card required on file at the time of intake.
