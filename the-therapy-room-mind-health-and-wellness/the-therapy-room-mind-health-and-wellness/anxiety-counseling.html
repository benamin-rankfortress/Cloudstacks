What is Emotionally Focused Therapy - Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) is a therapeutic approach designed to strengthen emotional bonds within romantic, familial, or personal relationships. Dr. Sue Johnson and Dr. Les Greenberg developed EFT with a foundation in attachment theory, emphasizing the innate human need for emotional connection and support. Initially developed for couples, EFT has evolved to include individuals seeking personal growth.

At its core, EFT aims to help individuals identify and understand their emotions, fostering a secure emotional connection with themselves and others. The therapy process typically involves exploring underlying emotional patterns, recognizing triggers, and promoting open communication.

The Key Principles Of Emotionally Focused Therapy Include - Attachment Theory: EFT is rooted in the idea that secure emotional attachments are fundamental to well-being. It recognizes the impact of early attachment experiences on adult relationships and emotional regulation.

How does online therapy work?

Online therapy with The Therapy Room program offers a convenient and effective way to receive mental health support from the comfort of your own home. Here's how it works:

  1. Scheduling: You start by scheduling a session with a licensed therapist or counselor using the "request an appointment" link on our website. You can typically choose a time and date that fits your schedule.

  2. Secure Video Calls: At the scheduled time, you'll log into your portal via our website and join a secure video call with your therapist. This virtual face-to-face interaction allows for a real-time conversation, just like an in-person session.

  3. Document Sharing: You can securely share documents, worksheets, or other materials with your therapist through the platform, enhancing the therapeutic process.

  4. Payment and Privacy: Payment is typically handled online, and your privacy and confidentiality are protected through secure encryption and adherence to privacy regulations.

Online therapy makes mental health care accessible and flexible, ensuring you can receive the support you need, wherever you are.
