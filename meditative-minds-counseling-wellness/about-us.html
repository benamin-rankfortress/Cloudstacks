About Us

Meditative Minds Counseling & Wellness

Meditative Minds Counseling is a holistic mental health therapy center that is dedicated to providing compassionate care to individuals and families seeking emotional well-being and healing. The center is staffed by a team of highly skilled and experienced holistic practitioners who are passionate about helping clients address all aspects of the self, including the mind, body, and spirit. At Meditative Minds Counseling, we believe that emotional well-being is essential to living a fulfilling life. We offer a range of services to help clients achieve their goals, including mental health counseling, trauma-informed yoga, guided mindfulness, and breathwork, among others. About our team takes a holistic approach to mental health, combining traditional therapies with complementary approaches to promote emotional well-being and healing. We understand that counseling is just one aspect of the ways in which individuals can care for themselves, and we strive to offer a range of services to address the unique needs of each client. In addition to counseling, we also offer classes and workshops to teach skills and learn ways to release trauma stored in the body through trauma-informed yoga, guided mindfulness, and breathwork, among others.
