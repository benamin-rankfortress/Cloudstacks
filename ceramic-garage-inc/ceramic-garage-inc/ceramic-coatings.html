Ceramic Coatings

That’s when Ceramic Garage of Modesto, CA, was Established. The age of technology has influenced the upkeep of an automobile’s exterior. Anyone who has hand waxed a car should be shouting for joy and handing over their keys to these guys with the introduction of ceramic coating.

Ceramic coatings are silica-based sealants incredibly resistant to water, dirt, and environmental pollutants. What makes ceramic coating so special is that the liquid bonds with the exterior paint, a rough surface full of pores where dirt builds up, causing a damaging effect on your car’s paint. And when that dirt mixes with rain, it can create an acidic compound that damages the paint’s surface and lead to corrosion over time.

The nanoparticles in the ceramic coating are so small that they seal all those pores in the paint, creating an invisible layer that repels dirt and mud. So, when dust, pollen, or mud encounters your paint, they slide off as they have nothing to stick to.

The result, along with your paint protected against the elements, is a mirror-like finish.

Ceramic garage focuses on a showroom finish that turns heads. They also protect customers’ exterior paint by offering paint protection film, also known as ppf, for that added layer of protection.

PPF product is the guard dog for your paint finish. This “clear bra” is best to preserve a showroom-quality paint finish.

There are two options for this film: XPEL Ultimate Plus and XPEL Stealth. One shines, the other is flat, and both are exceptional. So, it makes sense that these treatments are not exclusive to automobiles. They also serve well for touring or street motorcycles if that’s your obsession.

What is a Ceramic Coating? - Ceramic Coating is a liquid polymer such as Ceramic Pro installed by hand on the exterior of a vehicle. The Coating chemically bonds with the vehicle’s factory paint, creating a layer of protection.

However, bear in mind that Ceramic Coating is not a substitute for Paint Protection Film. Paint protective film provides a comprehensive form of defense. Another way of explaining what it is. It’s equivalent to applying a premium wax to your car

So, Ceramic Coating creates a permanent or semi-permanent bond to your vehicle’s exterior paint. It will not wash away or break down and does not require repeated application every few months like car wax.

What Does a Ceramic Coating Do? - Ceramic Coating adds additional protection to your car’s exterior paint.

It gives your paint a glossy shine and keeps it looking like new. Ceramic Coating also makes your car easier to clean. Protection from UV Damage and Oxidization. If you live in California, it’s impossible to avoid the sun.

Exposure to the elements, especially the sun’s rays, and your paint will oxidize, resulting in dulled and faded paint. Applying Ceramic Coating protects your vehicle’s paint from the sun’s ultraviolet rays, thereby reducing the amount of oxidization. Ceramic Coating is like a protective shield for your car’s paint.
